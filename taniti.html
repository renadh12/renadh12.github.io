Rainforest Zip-lining Adventure

Adventure Ages 12+ Suitable

Description

Soar through the lush Tanitian rainforest on our thrilling zip-line course. Experience breathtaking views of the tropical canopy while spotting native wildlife and exotic birds. Our professionally designed course features multiple zip-lines of varying lengths, including our signature 500-foot canopy glider.

Professional guides ensure your safety with high-quality equipment and comprehensive training. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie or trying zip-lining for the first time, this unforgettable adventure offers the perfect blend of excitement and natural beauty.

Located in the pristine rainforest near Merriton Landing, our eco-friendly facility operates with minimal environmental impact, preserving Taniti's natural wonder for future generations.

What to Bring

  • Closed-toe shoes (required for safety)
  • Comfortable athletic clothing
  • Sunscreen and insect repellent
  • Water bottle (water provided on-site)
  • Camera or phone for photos (secure strap recommended)
  • Sense of adventure!

Safety & Requirements

  • Minimum age: 12 years old
  • Weight limit: 250 lbs (113 kg)
  • Good physical condition required
  • Not recommended for pregnant visitors
  • All safety equipment provided and inspected daily
  • Guides are certified and CPR trained

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled tour.

Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% fee. Tours may be rescheduled due to severe weather at no charge.
